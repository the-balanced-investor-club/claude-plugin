argument-hint: "[ticker, or leave empty]"

Load the `stock-tracker` skill and help the user manage their Stock Trackers — add or remove an instrument, create a tracker, or browse the official ones curated by the research team.

If a ticker is provided, resolve it and check whether it is already tracked. Otherwise show them what they own and follow.
